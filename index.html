Exam Muscles

studybot is an online quiz generator that simulates exam conditions in an fun environment. The questions are based on past examination papers as these questions are most reflective of what the future questions may be.

Exam technique can be developed through repetitions in the same manner as going to the gym. Each time you go work out if you aim to beat your previous best by a small amount. Overtime this develops muscle memory and technique in order to execute.

About Us

studybot helps students develop strong examination technique through quizes based on past examination questions.

Exam technique like all other skills can be developed through training. It is essential to have good exam skills to be able to translate understanding into raw marks.
